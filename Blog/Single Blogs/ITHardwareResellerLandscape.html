    Price Competition:

    This is a big challenge for any hardware reseller in the digital industry as the market is growing, customers are more informed and tend to explore multiple sources to purchase their desired product, this factor has initiated the price war, every shop owner or reseller is struggling to increase their buyers and offer minimum prices, this intense and high competition has already impacted the price margins. Most customers are focusing on economical purchasing of the products, thus after so much hard work, profit margins are not satisfying for most IT hardware resellers.


    Dependency on Suppliers:

    As we know resellers are the middleman between manufacturers and end-users, so they are highly dependable on the distributor and manufacturer, any kind of disruptions and irregularities in the supply chain can impact the market of the reseller, and their name can be at stake because user mostly blame the reseller for any kind of faulty, damaged or old version products.


    Changing Trends:

    The IT landscape is continuously evolving and manufacturers are always in a race to produce different, latest, and upgraded versions of their products, These technological advancements can be challenging and force the resellers to adapt to the changes, to ensure the up-to-date solutions to remain competitive and reputable in the market.

    Resellers have to maintain their business models even if they sell products at thin margins.

    Customers want comprehensive support, warranties, and post-sales services. These services can drain the resellers and service providers, and cost big to small structured and new resellers.As if they do not offer such additional services to customers, they can not grow and become successful in the market. this is the big con for any resellers and new persons planning to join this industry as a reseller.
